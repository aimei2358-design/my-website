01About

Christina Perng

I am a real estate agent at Compass Menlo Park. Having lived in the Bay Area for 23 years and currently residing in Palo Alto, I have personally experienced every change this land has seen—from the rise of the housing market to the growth of communities. This deep familiarity with the area allows me to help clients better assess a neighborhood, a street, or even a single home, to determine if it's truly the right fit for their life.

Over 15 years, I have specialized in residential real estate services across Palo Alto, Menlo Park, and cities throughout the Bay Area, having assisted over 200 families in completing home purchases, sales, or investment transactions. My clients range from first-time buyers of young families, to move-up buyers, to investors seeking opportunities—I approach every one of them with the same care and expertise, accompanying them every step of the way in every important decision.

In addition to real estate, I also have a background in Staging design. This allows me to provide effective placement advice when helping clients sell their homes, giving buyers a fresh perspective and helping properties stand out in the market.
