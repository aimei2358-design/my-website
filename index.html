01About

Christina Perng

I am a real estate agent at Compass Menlo Park. Having lived in the Bay Area for 23 years and currently residing in Palo Alto, I have personally experienced every change this land has seen—from the rise of the housing market to the growth of communities. This familiarity allows me to help clients better assess a neighborhood, a street, or even a single home.

Over 15 years, I have specialized in residential real estate across Palo Alto, Menlo Park, and cities throughout the Bay Area, having assisted over 200 families in completing home purchases, sales, or investment transactions. I approach every client with the same care and expertise, accompanying them every step of the way.

In addition to real estate, I also have a background in Staging design, allowing me to provide effective advice when helping clients sell their homes and stand out in the market.

Buying, selling, investing—every transaction represents an important decision for a family. I hope to be a trusted partner for you on this journey.
