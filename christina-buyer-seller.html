The Buying Process 買房步驟

1

Get Pre-Approved for a Loan

Before you start looking, know your budget. I can connect you with trusted local mortgage professionals to get you pre-approved quickly and confidently.

2

確認需求，開始搜尋

We'll discuss your must-haves, budget, preferred neighborhoods, and timeline. I'll send you curated listings daily and arrange viewings on your schedule.

Once your offer is accepted, I guide you through inspections, contingency review, and the escrow process — keeping everything on track until you get your keys.

My service doesn't stop at closing. I'll connect you with trusted contractors, movers, and service providers — and I'm always here for questions long after the deal is done.

Christina's Buyer Tips 買家小提示

  • Get pre-approved before you start looking — sellers take pre-approved buyers more seriously 在看房前先取得貸款預批
  • Review disclosures carefully — I'll explain every clause and flag potential issues 仔細閱讀揭露文件
  • Don't overlook the neighborhood — schools, commute, and community matter as much as the home 不要忽略社區環境
  • Be flexible on closing dates — sometimes that wins the deal over a higher price 彈性的交割日期有時比高出價更有效
  • Trust your agent — I've seen 40-offer situations; I know what it takes to win 信任你的房仲，我有豐富的競價經驗

Frequently Asked Questions

常見問題解答
Q

How competitive is the Bay Area market right now?

現在灣區市場競爭激烈嗎？

The Bay Area remains one of the most competitive real estate markets in the US. Well-priced homes in desirable areas often receive multiple offers. I help buyers prepare strategically and sellers position themselves to attract strong offers.

Q

Do I need to be pre-approved before looking at homes?

看房前需要先取得貸款預批嗎？

Yes — strongly recommended. Sellers in the Bay Area expect buyers to be pre-approved. It also helps you know exactly what you can afford and move quickly when you find the right home.

Q

How long does it typically take to buy a home?

買房通常需要多長時間？

From offer acceptance to closing is typically 21–30 days in the Bay Area. The house-hunting phase varies widely — some clients find their home in weeks, others take a few months. I work at your pace.

Q

Can you help Chinese-speaking clients through the whole process?

您可以全程用中文服務嗎？

Absolutely. I am fully bilingual in Chinese and English and provide complete service in both languages — from initial consultation through closing and beyond. 我提供全程中英文雙語服務。

Q

What areas do you serve?

您服務哪些區域？

I specialize in Palo Alto, Menlo Park, Mountain View, Cupertino, Saratoga, Sunnyvale, Santa Clara, San Jose, Fremont, and surrounding Bay Area cities — both Peninsula and East Bay.
